"Cheffrey" Jeff Greco 

 

In need of an all vegan entree for you catering event? How about a menu that is totally without gluten or soy allergens? Look no further than "Cheffrey", Chef Jeffrey Greco . His signature style, influenced by Italian, South Indian, Central American, and Eastern U.S. cuisine, will take your tongue through a culinary journey you won't soon forget.

Starting at a small tavern in rural Pennsylvania as a dishwasher, Jeffrey began learning the ropes with local beef burgers, chicken wings, specialty sauces and homemade soups. After a year as kitchen manager, Jeff moved with his then girlfriend to central North Carolina and got a job at Duke University. Five years on the university dining hall circuit between Duke and Elon University has led Chef Greco to a higher leadership position at Elon Dining.

Currently, Chef Greco is a Chef de Partie at Elon University's Lakeside Dining Hall. Additionally, he is the lead cook at the allergen-free cooking station, Allgood. This seperate cooking area features a full entree and made-to-order station that has menus without the top nine allergens. This past year as included four specialty "Chef's Table" menus:

  •  Peruvian Grilled Chicken, Arroz Chaufa with  Succotash and Aji Verde
  • Allergen-Friendly Mardi Gras with Dirty Rice Stuffed Bell Peppers, Heart of Palm "Crab" Stuffed Mushrooms, and Made-to-Order Oyster Mushroom Etouffe Bar    
  • Allergen-Friendly and Vegan Barbeque with Strawberry Balsamic Jackfruit, Watermelon Daiya Salad, Bread & Butter Pickles,   Gluten-free Pasta Salad, and Charred Corn on the Cob
  • Allergen-Friendly Indian Curry Bar with Chicken Rogan Josh, Roast Aloo Gobi, Dal Makhan, and Spiced Pakora Latkes with Oatmilk Yogurt Raita
